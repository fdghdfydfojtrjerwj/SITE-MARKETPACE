MacBook Neo

300$

• Design & Mobility: An ultra-thin and lightweight gadget. When closed, it looks almost identical to a classic MacBook. However, you can detach the screen at any moment to use it as a standalone tablet.

• Display: A stunning, crisp touchscreen (Liquid Retina) that is perfect not just for coding or typing, but also for drawing, browsing, and navigation with your fingers or an Apple Pencil.

• Performance: Powered by desktop-class Apple Silicon (M-series) chips, this "transformer" matches the speed of traditional Apple laptops. It easily handles web development, design, and heavy multitasking.

• Ergonomics: The keyboard features great key travel, backlighting, and a built-in trackpad that perfectly replicates the Mac experience. The unique "floating" magnetic cantilever design allows you to adjust the screen to your perfect viewing angle.

• Operating System: Driven by iPadOS, featuring Stage Manager for seamless multi-window multitasking, while maintaining the speed and simplicity of a mobile OS.
